### Title
`calculate_next_l2_gas_price_for_fin` retroactively applies new-version `gas_target` to the previous block's gas usage at Starknet version upgrade boundaries — (`crates/apollo_consensus_orchestrator/src/fee_market/mod.rs`)

### Summary

The EIP-1559 L2 gas price calculation always reads `gas_target` and `gas_price_max_change_denominator` from `VersionedConstants::latest_constants()`. When the sequencer binary is upgraded to a new Starknet version that carries a different `gas_target`, the very next call to `calculate_next_l2_gas_price_for_fin` evaluates the **previous block's** gas usage against the **new** target — exactly the "new rate applied to the past interval" pattern from the external report.
