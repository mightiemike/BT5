### Title
`ValidResourceBounds` Protobuf Deserialization Collapses `AllResources` to `L1Gas` When l2_gas=0 and l1_data_gas=0, Causing Transaction Hash Mismatch Across P2P Boundary - (`crates/apollo_protobuf/src/converters/transaction.rs`)

### Summary

The protobuf converter `TryFrom<protobuf::ResourceBounds> for ValidResourceBounds` silently collapses an `AllResources` variant (with l2_gas=0 and l1_data_gas=0) into the `L1Gas` variant. Because `get_tip_resource_bounds_hash` produces a structurally different hash for these two variants — `AllResources` appends an extra `L1_DATA_GAS` element to the Poseidon hash chain while `L1Gas` does not — any node that recomputes a transaction hash from a protobuf-deserialized `InvokeTransactionV3`
