### Title
`DepositAllowlistExtension` gates on `owner` not `sender`, letting any unprivileged caller bypass the deposit allowlist via the official `MetricOmmPoolLiquidityAdder` - (File: metric-periphery/contracts/extensions/DepositAllowlistExtension.sol)

---

### Summary

`DepositAllowlistExtension.beforeAddLiquidity` silently discards the `sender` argument (the actual payer / `msg.sender` of `addLiquidity`) and only checks `owner` (the position recipient). Because `MetricOmmPool.addLiquidity` explicitly supports `msg.sender ≠ owner`, any address that is not on the allowlist can add liquidity to a curated pool by supplying an allowlisted address as `owner`. The attack is executable through the production `MetricOmmPoolLiquidityAdder` periphery contract with no custom callbacks required.
