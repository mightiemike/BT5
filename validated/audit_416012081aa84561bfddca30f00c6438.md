### Title
`get_tip_resource_bounds_hash` Produces Divergent Transaction Hashes for `AllResources(x,0,0)` vs `L1Gas(x)` While Protobuf Converter Reconstructs Variant from Content — (File: `crates/starknet_api/src/transaction_hash.rs`)

---

### Summary

`get_tip_resource_bounds_hash` branches on the `ValidResourceBounds` enum variant to decide whether to include `L1_DATA_GAS` in the hash preimage. The protobuf converter for historical transactions (`TryFrom<protobuf::ResourceBounds> for ValidResourceBounds`) reconstructs this variant from content by checking whether `l2_gas` and `l1_data_gas` are zero. This creates a hash-domain collision: `AllResources(x, 0, 0)` and `L1Gas(x)` represent the same economic commitment but produce different transaction hashes. A transaction admitted through the gateway (always `AllResources`)
