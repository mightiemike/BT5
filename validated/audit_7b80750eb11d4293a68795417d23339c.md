### Title
Unconditional `minDepositRateMultiplierX18` Applied to Both Multipliers Inflates Net Liquidity Without Token Inflow — (`core/contracts/SpotEngineState.sol`)

---

### Summary

`_updateState` applies `minDepositRateMultiplierX18` to **both** `cumulativeDepositsMultiplierX18` and `cumulativeBorrowsMultiplierX18` every time a `SpotTick` is processed. Because deposits exceed borrows in any solvent pool, this unconditionally grows net liquidity (`totalDeposits − totalBorrows`) by `(minDepositRateMultiplierX18 − 1) × L` per tick with no corresponding token inflow to the Clearinghouse, violating the invariant that total withdrawable collateral ≤ tokens held.

---

### Finding Description

In `_updateState`, after normal interest is applied, the `minDepositRateX18` branch executes unconditionally whenever `minDepositRateX18 != 0`: [1](#0-0) 

Both multipliers are scaled by the same factor `m = minDepositRateMultiplierX18`:

```
cumulativeBorrowsMultiplierX18  *= m   // line 155-157
cumulativeDepositsMultiplierX18 *= m   // line 159-161
```

The code's own invariant comment (lines 102–113) states that the deposit rate multiplier is derived so that net liquidity is **preserved**: [2](#0-1) 

Applying the same factor `m` to both sides breaks this:

```
New net liquidity = (D × m) − (B × m) = (D − B) × m = L × m
```

The Clearinghouse still holds `L` tokens. The shortfall after one tick is:

```
shortfall = L × (m − 1)
```

This compounds every tick. After N ticks the shortfall is `L × (m^N − 1)`.

**Zero-utilization case (worst path):** When no one borrows, `utilizationRatioX18 == 0`, so `borrowerRateX18` is forced to zero (line 75) and no normal interest accrues at all. [3](#0-2) 

Yet the `minDepositRateX18` branch still fires, growing `cumulativeDepositsMultiplierX18` while the Clearinghouse token balance is unchanged. A depositor's withdrawable balance exceeds the tokens the Clearinghouse holds.

**The trigger is normal sequencer operation.** `updateStates` is `onlyEndpoint`: [4](#0-3) 

It is called by the sequencer via `SpotTick` in `EndpointTx`: [5](#0-4) 

The sequencer submits `SpotTick` as routine protocol operation — no compromise is required. The depositor only needs to deposit, wait for normal ticks, and withdraw.

---

### Impact Explanation

**Critical — unbacked collateral creation.** After enough ticks, the sum of all depositor withdrawal claims exceeds the tokens held by the Clearinghouse. The first depositors to withdraw receive more tokens than they deposited; later depositors and the protocol absorb the shortfall. At zero utilization the effect is immediate and unambiguous: every tick the depositor's claimable balance grows while the Clearinghouse balance is static.

---

### Likelihood Explanation

Any product configured with a nonzero `minDepositRateX18` is affected from the first `SpotTick` onward. The sequencer emits `SpotTick` continuously as part of normal operation. No special attacker capability is needed beyond depositing into such a product and submitting a standard withdrawal.

---

### Recommendation

The `minDepositRateX18` should act as a **floor**, not an additive bonus. The correct logic is:

1. Compute the natural deposit rate multiplier from borrower interest.
2. Only if the natural deposit rate is **below** `minDepositRateX18`, apply the difference — and only to `cumulativeDepositsMultiplierX18`, funded from the insurance fund or a designated subsidy source.
3. Never apply `minDepositRateMultiplierX18` to `cumulativeBorrowsMultiplierX18` unconditionally, as this inflates borrower debt without a corresponding token source for the net liquidity gap.

---

### Proof of Concept

```
Setup:
  product.minDepositRateX18 = 5e16  (5% annualized)
  Alice deposits 1000 USDC; no borrowers (utilization = 0)
  Clearinghouse.balanceOf(USDC) = 1000

After sequencer submits SpotTick with dt = 31536000 (1 year):
  minDepositRateMultiplierX18 ≈ e^0.05 ≈ 1.05127
  cumulativeDepositsMultiplierX18 *= 1.05127
  Alice.withdrawableBalance = 1000 × 1.05127 = 1051.27 USDC

Invariant check:
  Clearinghouse.balanceOf(USDC) = 1000
  Alice.withdrawableBalance     = 1051.27
  → shortfall = 51.27 USDC (unbacked)

Fuzz assertion that fails:
  assert Clearinghouse.balanceOf(token) >= 
         totalDepositsNormalized * cumulativeDepositsMultiplierX18
         - totalBorrowsNormalized * cumulativeBorrowsMultiplierX18
  // fails after any SpotTick when minDepositRateX18 != 0 and utilization < 1
```
