### Title
Perp Trading Fees Credited as Virtual PnL Instead of Actual USDC in `dumpFees()`, Making Them Permanently Uncollectable — (File: `core/contracts/OffchainExchange.sol`)

---

### Summary

`dumpFees()` distributes accumulated trading fees to `X_ACCOUNT`. For spot products it correctly credits actual USDC via `spotEngine.updateBalance`. For perp products it instead credits `X_ACCOUNT` with `vQuoteBalance` in the perp engine — virtual Pn
