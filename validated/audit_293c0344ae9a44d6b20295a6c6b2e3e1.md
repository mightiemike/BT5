### Title
Ongoing NLP Burns Reduce Shared Pool Capital, Hurting Future Earnings for All Remaining NLP Holders — (File: `core/contracts/Clearinghouse.sol`)

---

### Summary

When an NLP holder burns their tokens via `burnNlp`, the function reduces the quote balance of NLP pool subaccounts through `_applyNlpRebalance`. This directly reduces the capital available for earning trading fees, hurting future earnings for all remaining NLP holders. The burner also reduces the pool they remain part of — a direct structural analog to M-07, where variable side withdrawals reduce Lido staking balance and hurt all variable stakers.

---

### Finding Description

In `Clearinghouse.sol`, `burnNlp` executes the following sequence:

1. Burns `nlpAmount` NLP from the user's subaccount.
2. Mints `nlpAmount` NLP to `N_ACCOUNT`.
3. Computes `quoteAmount = nlpAmount * oraclePriceX18 - burnFee` and credits it to the user.
4. Reduces NLP pool subaccounts' quote balances by `quoteAmount` total via `_applyNlpRebalance`. [1](#0-0)
