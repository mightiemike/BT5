### Summary

`AccessKeyPermission::GasKeyFunctionCall(GasKeyInfo, FunctionCallPermission)` carries a `FunctionCallPermission` payload that encodes a `receiver_id` and `method_names` allowlist. For ordinary `FunctionCall` access keys these restrictions are enforced at transaction-verification time. For gas-key transactions the runtime dispatches to `verify_and_charge_gas_key_tx_ephemeral`, which validates gas-key balance and nonce but never inspects `function_call_permission()`. The `receiver_id` / `method_names` restrictions encoded in a `GasKeyFunctionCall` key are therefore silently ignored for every regular gas-key transaction, making `GasKeyFunctionCall` semantically equivalent to `GasKeyFullAccess`.
