### Title
`DISABLE_OP` Flag Dead-Code Wiring in `op_div`/`op_divmod`/`op_mod` Bypasses Intended Mempool Restriction - (File: `src/more_ops.rs`)

### Summary

The `DISABLE_OP` flag, which is part of `MEMPOOL_MODE`, is wired into `op_div`, `op_divmod`, `op_mod`, and their malachite variants with a threshold of `a0_len > 2048`. However, the unconditional size check immediately following — `a0_len > 256 || a1_len > 1024` — is always stricter. The `DISABLE_OP` guard can never trigger without the normal guard also triggering, making it permanently dead code. The intended mempool-mode restriction on these arithmetic operators is silently never applied.
